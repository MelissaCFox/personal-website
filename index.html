About Me

After a decade-long career in animal care, I made the decision to try something completely different.
I wanted to challenge myself. I wanted to solve problems. I wanted to create something amazing.
After tinkering around for a bit on my own, I discovered that I loved the puzzle-like quality of coding and decided to make the jump to a career in software development with the help of Nashville Software School.

I have been having so much fun with the new tools and skills that I have developed so far and I know that I will never get tired of learning new concepts and languages.

When I'm not coding you can find me exploring somewhere outside, on the trail with my dog, Moose, settling in for a night of gaming, or abruptly working in an obscure animal fact into an everyday conversation!
